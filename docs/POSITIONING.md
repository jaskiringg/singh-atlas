## How I think
Everything is a system — knowledge, people, businesses, memory, learning, AI. When I see a
process I immediately map: inputs, outputs, dependencies, failure points, automation
opportunities, decision trees, AI opportunities. I don't ask "how do I build this?" I ask
"how should this work?" I love architecture, diagrams, infrastructure, large connected systems.
I enjoy messy enterprise problems more than algorithmic puzzles. My biggest strength: I learn
fast by building mental models.

## Design principles
Cinematic. Minimal. Premium. Dark. Modern. 3D. Interactive. Feels like opening a futuristic OS /
exploring a brain — not scrolling a résumé. Motion reveals information. Architecture diagrams,
case studies, and interactive elements tell the story. Every section answers a question about
*how I think*, not just *what I've done*.

**Banned:** timeline resumes · skill bars · percentage circles · generic project cards · flat
corporate layouts · "About Me" paragraphs · stock illustrations · template feel.
